- Conceptually- "belief", "Faith", or strong feelings about a person, place or thing. Considered an adjacent form of power from Magic, wielded by Outsiders and bearers of a [[Divine Seed]].
  
- Trust can be channeled by Gods based on the belief that people have in them, in terms of available power to draw in, and the sorts of tasks that can be efficiently completed with that power.
  
- Trust can be collected from slaying Outsiders, who leave it behind in the form of crystal polyhedrons. Ingesting these polyhedrons allows an individual to bend the leftover trust to their will. For those with a [[Divine Seed]], this will manifest in predictable ways, for those without the results are unpredictable and potentially dangerous.
