
Crunchy Bell began preparations for their Arson plot targeting the castle's storehouse.

Jurggie and Hannah worked together, donning disguises to spread word among the merchants of a new tax to be imposed, as well as impersonating the town Crier making the same announcement.

Elsewhere, Mogus, Sniv, and Sneed worked on tracking down and identifying Ironwake company wagons so that they'd be easier to track later. In the process, Sneed overheard hushed conversations between cityfolk, hinting at a growing unrest related to the Eye watching through the Tear in the sky.

Tracking down the rumors to a crazed old man, they began to question him about the source of his rumors. When he began to act suspicious, they abducted him and pressed further- causing him to drop his disguise and reveal himself as a member of the Purple Cult.

The cultist was taken back to the First Cloud for further questioning, and with the help of Hannah's detect thoughts, the party was able to determine that the Cultist was working with 3 others to sow unrest in the region. Their ultimate goal is unknown, but it seems they were unaware of King Corso's plot.

Alan spent his time staking out the Queen's window, and was able to determine that whatever was impersonating her likely had some access to invisibility, leaving through the window at night and returning early in the morning.
