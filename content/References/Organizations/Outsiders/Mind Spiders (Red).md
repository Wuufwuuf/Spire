- Constantly "singing"- creatures that can hear the song are incapable of perceiving Mind Spiders or signs of their existence or activities.

- Individually- large creatures with a central body and abdomen comprised of human heads. The Abdomen head is constantly wailing the Mind Spider's song. All of their teeth are molars. The limbs appear to be humanoid fingers and thumbs. Their skin is eerily pale and fragile, often covered in sores and scabs.
  
- They hunt lazily, their prey is unable to perceive them. Upon capturing a victim they eat them alive.
  
- Not very dangerous individually, slow moving, moderately sturdy.
  
- When a sufficiently large colony of Mind Spiders realizes they're under threat, they can coordinate and fuse bodies to increase their combat ability- permanently becoming a new type of creature.
